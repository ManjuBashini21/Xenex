What industries does Xenex Engineering serve?

We specialize in industries such as Water Treatment, Oil & Gas, Power Plants, and Process Industrial Engineering, offering tailored fiberglass solutions to meet diverse needs.

What services does Xenex provide?

Our services include GRP/FRP engineering, installation, repair, lamination, coating, testing, inspection, and in-house training for fiberglass systems.

Why should I choose Xenex over competitors?

With over 5+ years of experience, a qualified team, cost-effective solutions, and a commitment to quality, Xenex ensures reliable and durable results tailored to your requirements.

Do you offer customized fiberglass solutions?

Yes, we provide bespoke solutions for FRP tanks, pipes, coatings, and more, ensuring they meet specific project and industry needs.

Does Xenex provide training for fiberglass system handling?

Yes, we offer in-house qualification training and practical workshops for employees and partners to ensure expertise in project execution.
