About Xenex Engineering & Process Pte. Ltd.

Xenex Engineering & Process Pte. Ltd. is a trusted provider of innovative fiberglass solutions with over 5+ years of expertise in Water Treatment, Oil & Gas, Power Plants, and Process Engineering. We specialize in high-quality GRP/FRP systems, offering design, installation, and maintenance services with a focus on durability, cost-effectiveness, and customer satisfaction.
