GRP/FRP Engineering Solutions


FRP Pipe & Tank Fabrication & Installation

Custom FRP Piping Systems – Durable, corrosion-resistant solutions for industrial applications.

FRP Tank Fabrication – Leak-proof, chemical-resistant storage tanks for chemicals, water, and oil.

On-Site & Off-Site Installation – Expertise in modular fabrication and field assembly.

High-Pressure & Large-Diameter Piping – Designed for extreme environments and industrial processes.

FRP Lamination & Lining

Structural FRP Lamination – Enhancing durability of pipes, tanks, and industrial structures.

FRP Tank & Pipeline Lining – Corrosion-resistant lining for hazardous chemical and wastewater storage.

FRP Coating for Steel & Concrete Surfaces – Protecting infrastructure from wear and tear.

Custom FRP Bonding & Jointing – High-performance adhesion solutions for long-term durability.

FRP Flooring & Roof Lamination

Industrial Flooring Systems – Slip-resistant, fire-retardant, and corrosion-proof FRP flooring.

Marine & Offshore Decking – High-strength, weather-resistant solutions for harsh environments.

Chemical-Resistant Flooring – Ideal for refineries, chemical plants, and power plants.

Waterproof FRP Roof Coatings – Long-lasting protection against extreme weather conditions.

FRP Structural & Custom Fabrication

FRP Platforms & Walkways – Lightweight, strong, and maintenance-free industrial structures.

FRP Cable Trays & Ducting Systems – Electrical and ventilation solutions for industrial plants.

FRP Handrails, Ladders & Covers – Safe and durable solutions for industrial and offshore applications.

FRP Testing & Quality Assurance

Hydrostatic & Pressure Testing – Ensuring pipeline and tank integrity.

Load & Structural Strength Testing – Evaluating FRP durability and safety.

Industries We Serve

Marine & Offshore: Seawater intake piping, offshore platforms, corrosion-resistant gratings, and handrails.

Chemical & Oil & Gas: FRP process piping, storage tanks, chemical-resistant linings, and fire-resistant flooring.

Power Plants & Refineries: Cooling water systems, acid-resistant flooring, ducting, and structural reinforcements.

Water Treatment & Construction: FRP pipes for desalination, waterproof tank linings, roofing, and paneling.
